What is Keeda Makauda?

Through our designs, we bring to you, the unknown and the unseen . Using photographs by Ashir Kumar, Macro-wildlife photographer, we have created illustrations highlighting the idiosyncrasies of various insects, butterflies and bugs. We bring to you the designs /patterns your naked eye might have missed.
